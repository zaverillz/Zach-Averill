My Story

I'm passionate about creating outdoor gear that performs when it matters most. With a background in mechanical engineering and a love for mountain adventures, I bridge the gap between technical innovation and real-world performance.

My approach combines rigorous engineering principles with extensive field testing. Whether it's developing safer climbing protection or more efficient backcountry skiing equipment, I believe the best products come from understanding both the technical requirements and the human experience.

When I'm not in the lab or at the drawing board, you'll find me testing gear in the mountains - because the best way to design outdoor equipment is to live the adventures it's meant to enable.

🧗

Z4 Camalot Field Testing

Yosemite Valley, CA

📷 Testing Documentation

Engineering Innovation

Challenge: Create a lighter, more reliable camming device with improved trigger mechanics and expanded size range for modern climbing routes.

Solution: Redesigned cam profile geometry, implemented dual-axle system, and conducted extensive field testing across various rock types and placements.

Result: 25% weight reduction, 40% improvement in trigger reliability, and expanded protection range from 0.4" to 6" in a single series.
